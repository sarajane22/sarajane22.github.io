The Rule, Not the Exception: Why Fiction Novels Disappoint Us In the End and Why That Isn't Such A Bad Thing

20 mins

With this blog post, I take the reader through the different stipulations of fiction novels and how they may set specific expectations for real life. I explore how these expectations may lead to some disappointment in real life...but also how these disappointments may have a side to them that don't necessarily make the experience negative. Give it a read!.

Cancel Culture: The Permanent Repercussions

20 mins

In this few minute discussion, the idea of cancel culture and how the "rules" of this concept changes depending on the setting, such as the societal perception for a 'celebrity' in comparison to a college student. With this short podcast, we investigate the common misconceptions and miscommunications cancel culture encourages in our modern day world, as well as the negative aftermath such a concept stimulates behind the scenes. Check it out!
